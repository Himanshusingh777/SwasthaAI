üéØ Scheme Overview

The Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY) is a government initiative launched on January 1, 2017, under Section 4 of the National Food Security Act, 2013. This scheme provides financial assistance to pregnant and lactating women to improve maternal and child health outcomes while compensating for wage loss during pregnancy and childbirth.

üöÄ How to Apply

Application Process:

  1. Visit Anganwadi Center or Health Center - Register your pregnancy early
  2. Contact ASHA/ANM Workers - They will help with the application process
  3. Fill Application Form - Complete Form 1A for registration
  4. Submit Required Documents - Provide all necessary certificates and proofs
  5. Follow Up for Installments - Complete required health check-ups and submit additional forms (1B and 1C) for subsequent installments
  6. Bank Account Linking - Ensure your bank account is linked with Aadhaar for direct benefit transfer

üì± Online Registration
