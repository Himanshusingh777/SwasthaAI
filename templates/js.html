üéØ Scheme Overview

Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY) is a safe motherhood intervention launched in 2005 under the National Health Mission (NHM). It is a 100% centrally sponsored scheme that integrates cash assistance with delivery and post-delivery care to promote institutional delivery among pregnant women belonging to Below Poverty Line (BPL) families.

üéØ Objectives & Benefits

  • Reduce maternal and neonatal mortality by promoting institutional delivery
  • Provide cash assistance to partially compensate for loss of wages
  • Enable poor families to meet direct and indirect costs of accessing health services
  • Strengthen the delivery system through capacity building and training
  • Create a supportive environment for promoting safe delivery practices
  • Improve rural-urban and inter-state equity in accessing skilled attendance at delivery
